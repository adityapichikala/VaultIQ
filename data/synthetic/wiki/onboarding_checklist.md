# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Owner:** People Operations  
**ACL:** hr, leadership

---

## Welcome to BigCorp!

This checklist covers everything you need to complete in your first 30 days. Your onboarding buddy and manager will help you through this process.

## Week 1: Getting Started

### Day 1 — Welcome Day

- [ ] Pick up laptop and ID badge from IT desk (Building A, Floor 2)
- [ ] Complete I-9 and tax forms with HR (Room 204)
- [ ] Set up corporate email (firstname.lastname@bigcorp.com)
- [ ] Activate VPN using the token provided by IT
- [ ] Join mandatory Slack channels: #general, #announcements, #your-team
- [ ] Meet your onboarding buddy (assigned by your manager)
- [ ] Attend the 2 PM "BigCorp 101" session (Building B, Auditorium)

### Day 2-3 — Access & Tools

- [ ] Request access to the following systems:

| System | How to Request | Approval By |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Jira | IT Portal → "New Access" | Auto-approved |
| GitHub Enterprise | IT Portal → "Dev Tools" | Team Lead |
| AWS Console | IT Portal → "Cloud Access" | Engineering Manager |
| Confluence | Auto-provisioned | — |
| Slack (private channels) | Ask team lead | Team Lead |
| PagerDuty | IT Portal → "On-Call" | SRE Manager |
| DataDog | IT Portal → "Observability" | Team Lead |

- [ ] Install required development tools (see Engineering Setup Guide)
- [ ] Clone the team's repositories and run the local dev environment
- [ ] Complete the "Security Awareness Training" module (2 hours, on LMS)

### Day 4-5 — Team Integration

- [ ] 1:1 with your direct manager (30 min)
- [ ] Meet each team member (15-min coffee chats)
- [ ] Review team's Confluence space and recent sprint docs
- [ ] Shadow a senior team member on their current task
- [ ] Attend your first standup

## Week 2: Deeper Dive

- [ ] Complete "Code Standards & Review Process" training
- [ ] Submit your first PR (even if it's a README fix)
- [ ] Attend the architecture overview session with your team lead
- [ ] Set up your local testing environment
- [ ] Read the last 3 ADRs for your team's service

## Week 3-4: Contributing

- [ ] Pick up your first Jira ticket (your manager will assign a starter task)
- [ ] Complete the "Deployment Process" training (self-paced, 1 hour)
- [ ] Have a 30-day check-in with your manager
- [ ] Fill out the "Onboarding Feedback" survey
- [ ] Update your profile on the internal People Directory
