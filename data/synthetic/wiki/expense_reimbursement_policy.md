# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Owner:** Finance — Accounts Payable  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025  
**ACL:** all-employees

---

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all BigCorp employees incurring business-related expenses. Contractors and vendors follow separate procurement policies.

## 2. Eligible Expenses

| Category | Daily Limit | Requires Pre-Approval |
|----------|------------|----------------------|
| Domestic travel (flight) | ₹15,000 | Yes, if > ₹10,000 |
| International travel (flight) | ₹80,000 | Always |
| Hotel — Tier 1 cities | ₹6,000/night | No |
| Hotel — Tier 2/3 cities | ₹4,000/night | No |
| Hotel — International | $200/night | Yes |
| Meals — domestic travel | ₹1,500/day | No |
| Meals — international travel | $75/day | No |
| Client entertainment | ₹5,000/event | Yes, VP approval |
| Software/tools subscription | ₹2,000/month | Team lead |
| Books & learning | ₹5,000/quarter | No |
| Conference registration | ₹25,000/event | Engineering Manager |

## 3. Submission Process

1. **Submit within 30 days** of incurring the expense
2. Attach original receipts (photo or scan) — no receipt, no reimbursement
3. Use the Expense Portal: `https://expenses.bigcorp.internal`
4. Select the correct cost center and GL code
5. Manager approval is auto-routed based on amount:
   - < ₹5,000: Team lead
   - ₹5,000 – ₹25,000: Engineering Manager
   - > ₹25,000: VP + Finance

## 4. Reimbursement Timeline

- Approved expenses are processed in the **next payroll cycle** (15th or 30th of the month)
- International expenses are converted at the RBI reference rate on the date of the transaction
- GST input credit is claimed by Finance — always get GST invoices when possible

## 5. Non-Reimbursable Items

- Personal travel or hotel upgrades
- Alcohol (unless part of approved client entertainment)
- First-class flights (only business class for international > 6 hours, with VP approval)
- Gym memberships (covered separately under wellness benefit)
- Home office equipment beyond the initial ₹25,000 allowance
- Fines, penalties, or parking tickets

## 6. Corporate Credit Card

Senior managers (L6+) are eligible for a corporate credit card. Usage rules:

- Only for business expenses — personal use will result in disciplinary action
- Reconcile all transactions within 15 days of statement date
- Lost/stolen cards: call HDFC immediately at 1800-XXX-XXXX, then notify Finance
