## Health Insurance

BigCorp provides comprehensive health coverage through HDFC ERGO for all full-time employees from Day 1.

### Coverage Details

| Benefit | Coverage |
|---------|---------|
| Sum Insured | ₹10,00,000 per family per year |
| Covered Members | Self + Spouse + Up to 2 Children + Parents/In-Laws |
| Room Rent | No sub-limits (any room category) |
| Pre-existing Conditions | Covered after 1 year waiting period |
| Maternity | ₹75,000 (normal) / ₹1,00,000 (C-section) after 9 months |
| Day Care Procedures | Covered (200+ procedures) |
| Mental Health | Covered up to ₹50,000/year |

### How to Use

1. For planned hospitalization: Inform hr-benefits@bigcorp.internal 48 hours in advance
2. For emergencies: Get treated first, submit claim within 15 days
3. Cashless network: 5,000+ hospitals (check list at hdfc.ergo.com/network)
4. Reimbursement claims: Submit on HR portal → Benefits → Insurance Claims

## Wellness Programs

### Mental Health Support
- **Manah Wellness partnership**: 8 free therapy sessions per year (in-person or video)
- Book via: manah.co/bigcorp (use your employee email)
- Completely confidential — HR has no access to session details
- Additional sessions: 50% subsidy on regular rates

### Fitness & Wellness
- ₹5,000/quarter reimbursement for gym, yoga, sports, or fitness apps
- Submit receipts on HR portal → Benefits → Wellness
- Annual health checkup: Free for employees, ₹2,500 for dependents (partnered with Apollo)

### Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- 24/7 helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX (toll-free)
- Services: stress management, financial counseling, legal advice, relationship support
- Free for all employees and their family members
