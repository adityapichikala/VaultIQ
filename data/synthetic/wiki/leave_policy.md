## 1. Leave Types and Entitlements

| Leave Type | Days/Year | Carry Forward | Encashment |
|-----------|----------|--------------|------------|
| Earned Leave (EL) | 18 | Up to 10 days | Yes, at separation |
| Casual Leave (CL) | 7 | No | No |
| Sick Leave (SL) | 12 | Up to 5 days | No |
| Maternity Leave | 26 weeks | N/A | N/A |
| Paternity Leave | 10 days | N/A | N/A |
| Bereavement Leave | 5 days | N/A | N/A |
| Marriage Leave | 3 days | N/A | N/A |
| Comp-Off | Earned per extra day worked | Must use within 30 days | No |

## 2. Leave Application Process

1. Apply on the HRMS portal at least **3 working days in advance** for planned leave
2. Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate
3. Manager must approve/reject within 2 working days
4. If manager doesn't respond, leave is **auto-approved** after 48 hours

## 4. Work From Home ≠ Leave

WFH is not a leave type. If you're unwell and cannot work, apply for sick leave. Working from home while mildly unwell is discouraged — please rest and recover.

## 5. Leave Without Pay (LWP)

If you exhaust all leave balances, additional days off are marked as LWP. LWP impacts:
- Monthly salary (deducted pro-rata)
- Annual bonus eligibility (>10 days LWP = partial bonus)
- Probation extension (if on probation)
