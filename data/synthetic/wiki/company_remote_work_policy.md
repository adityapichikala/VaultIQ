# Remote Work Policy — BigCorp Global

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025  
**Policy Owner:** HR Operations  
**Last Reviewed:** March 1, 2026  
**ACL:** all-employees

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines BigCorp's approach to remote and hybrid work arrangements. All full-time employees in good standing are eligible to apply for remote work, subject to manager approval and role suitability.

## 2. Eligibility

Employees must meet **all** of the following criteria:

- Completed probation period (minimum 6 months)
- Performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or above in last review cycle
- Role classified as "remote-eligible" by department head
- No active Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

### 2.1 Roles Not Eligible

The following roles require on-site presence and are **not eligible** for full remote:

| Department | Roles |
|-----------|-------|
| Facilities | All maintenance and security staff |
| Lab Operations | Hardware engineers, QA testers with physical equipment |
| Executive Office | Executive assistants (hybrid only) |
| Customer Support | Tier-1 phone agents in Hyderabad center |

## 3. Work Arrangements

| Arrangement | Days in Office | Who Can Apply |
|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| Full Remote | 0 days/week | Senior engineers, staff+ with 2+ years tenure |
| Hybrid A | 2 days/week (Tue, Thu) | Most eligible employees |
| Hybrid B | 3 days/week (Mon, Wed, Fri) | New hires after probation |
| On-Site | 5 days/week | Non-eligible roles |

## 4. Equipment and Expenses

BigCorp provides a one-time **₹25,000 home-office setup allowance** for approved remote workers. This covers:

- Ergonomic chair or standing desk converter
- Monitor (up to 27")
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls

Monthly internet reimbursement: **₹1,500/month** (requires broadband speed ≥ 50 Mbps).

## 5. Core Hours

All remote employees must be available during **core hours: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM IST**, regardless of their location. Meetings should be scheduled within core hours. Async communication is encouraged outside core hours.

## 6. Security Requirements

- Company-issued laptop must be used for all work
- VPN must be active when accessing internal systems
- No public Wi-Fi without VPN
- Screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- No work from co-working spaces without IT Security approval (submit form HR-SEC-042)

## 7. Revocation

Remote work privileges may be revoked with 2 weeks' notice if:

- Performance drops below "Meets Expectations"
- Security policy violations occur
- Business needs require on-site presence
- Employee fails to attend mandatory in-office days without prior approval

## 8. Contact

For questions, contact **hr-remote@bigcorp.internal** or raise a ticket on the HR portal under "Remote Work" category.
